ℹ️ Our Story

About Divyam Turfs Booking

Making sports booking simple and accessible in Mumbai

Our Story

Built From a Real Problem

Finding a good turf in Mumbai shouldn't take 2 hours of calls and cancelled WhatsApp messages. As college students who played football every weekend, we faced this frustration firsthand.

Divyam Turfs started as a college project with one simple goal: bring all Mumbai's best sports venues into one clean, easy-to-use platform where you can browse, compare, and book instantly.

Today, we've partnered with 55+ premium venues across Mumbai — from Andheri to Navi Mumbai — and helped over 15,000 players book the perfect slot without any hassle.
